How It Works

Recycle in 4 Simple Steps

From scan to reward in under 60 seconds — responsible e-waste disposal has never been this easy.

1

Scan QR Code

Open the SmartSort app or scan the QR code on any kiosk to authenticate and begin your drop-off session.

2

Drop Your Device

Place your old phone, charger, battery, or small electronic into the kiosk's secure intake compartment.

3

AI Sorts It

On-device TensorFlow Lite models classify the e-waste type, assess weight, and route it to the correct bin in real time.

4

Earn Rewards

Receive SmartSort credits instantly — redeem them at partner stores or donate to environmental causes.

Our Stack

Built on Open Technology

A robust, edge-first architecture designed for reliability in Indian urban conditions.

Raspberry Pi 4 Edge Node

Each kiosk runs a Raspberry Pi 4 as its brain — handling inference, sensor data, and cloud sync with minimal power draw.

TensorFlow Lite AI

On-device classification of 20+ e-waste categories using quantized TFLite models optimized for edge inference at 15 FPS.

Firebase Cloud Platform

Real-time database sync, user authentication, cloud functions, and analytics — all powered by Firebase for 99.9% uptime.

QR & RFID Authentication

Dual-mode auth ensures every drop-off is tracked. QR for app users, RFID for institutional deployments and repeat visitors.

Load Cell Sensors

Precision weight measurement validates each item, helps detect fraud, and provides accurate recycling metrics.

Wi-Fi & GSM Connectivity

Dual-band connectivity with automatic failover ensures kiosks stay online even in areas with intermittent Wi-Fi coverage.
